How FixLens works

Upload a photo, describe what's going on, or add a short voice note. FixLens sends everything securely to the AI brain and returns a clear explanation before you call a professional.

What it can help with

  • Car and engine issues
  • Home appliances and utilities
  • Workshop tools and everyday problems
  • Pipes, wires, and wall studs (with compatible sensors or clear photos)

Safety & policy

FixLens gives guidance and explanations only. It does not repair or perform any physical work. Always follow local safety codes and consult a licensed professional for critical issues.
