How FixLens works

1. Capture

Take a clear photo or short video of the problem—car engine, strange leak, broken device, anything in the real world.

2. Describe

Add a short description or voice note about what you see, hear, or smell. The more detail you share, the smarter the diagnosis.

3. Understand

FixLens combines vision + language AI to give you a clear, human-friendly explanation and possible next steps.

Safety & legal policy

FixLens does not repair or replace anything. It provides information only — like a smart “second opinion” based on your photo and description.

FixLens can:

  • Help you understand what might be wrong.
  • Suggest what to check or ask a professional about.
  • Explain risks in simple, human language.

FixLens cannot:

  • Guarantee that the diagnosis is 100% correct in every case.
  • Replace certified mechanics, electricians, plumbers, or any licensed expert.
  • Take responsibility for any damage caused by ignoring professional advice.

Important: Always use FixLens as guidance only. If something is dangerous, expensive, or safety-critical, talk to a qualified professional before taking action.
